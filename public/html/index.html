Hi, Olivia here!

HOPBAG* was born with the passion for handmade bags in my home studio. I grew up by crafting and drawing with the family from my mom's side. My vision for design is inspired by the beautiful nature and my lovely kids.

The mission of HOPBAG* is to create environmentally-friendly well-organized bags for every day use. I design with simplicity and convenience in mind. Enough pockets inside-out help to organize all your odds-and-ends for travelling, working and crafting. Non-toxic wax cotton canvas makes all HOPBAG* easy to clean and sturdy.

Quality craftsmanship in every detail is one of the key features of HOPBAG*. I focus on handcrafting bags that will look like new for years. The process of crafting always starts from selecting high-quality and nontoxic materials. Strong and neat stitches are must-have features on all HOPBAG*.

When you purchase a HOPBAG*, you also purchase our promise of 100% customer satisfaction. I stand behind our bags and services.

HOPBAG* 's original designs will make you proud. No other bag is made like HOPBAG*. Your satisfaction is my priority.
